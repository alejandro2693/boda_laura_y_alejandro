Byliner Script is a flowing handwritten typeface with a calm, confident rhythm.

The letters are drawn with a steady hand and a natural slant, balancing smooth curves with slightly firm strokes. It feels casual without being messy, and expressive without becoming loud. Capitals are open and friendly, while the lowercase keeps a consistent movement that makes longer words easy to read. The script has a relaxed elegance, like a practiced signature written without rush.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Designed by Mans Greback, Byliner Script was created with a focus on natural handwriting rather than decoration. The goal was to capture a smooth writing flow that feels personal and familiar, while still being dependable and clean in everyday use.
